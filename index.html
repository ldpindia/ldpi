About Us

The Liberal Democratic Party of India (LDPI) was founded in March 2024 with the aim of promoting liberalism, economic liberalization, and cultural progressivism. Our vision is to create a prosperous, inclusive, and secular India that values individual freedoms and encourages social harmony.

We believe in the importance of a strong democratic framework that respects the rights of every citizen. Our party's ideology emphasizes the need for a balanced approach to governance that supports both economic growth and social justice. We strive to empower citizens through education, entrepreneurship, and technological advancement.

As a party, we are committed to transparent governance, responsible leadership, and fostering a culture of innovation. Our leadership, headed by Anurag Sharma, is dedicated to engaging with citizens and addressing their concerns. We aim to represent the voices of the marginalized and ensure that every Indian has the opportunity to thrive.

Policies

The LDPI is committed to implementing policies that promote economic growth, social equity, and cultural progress. Here are some of our key policy areas:

  • Economic Liberalization

    We advocate for open markets and foreign direct investment (FDI) to create jobs and boost economic growth. Our policies aim to simplify regulations for businesses, encourage entrepreneurship, and promote fair competition.

  • Education Reform

    We believe that education is the cornerstone of a prosperous society. Our policy initiatives include improving access to quality education, enhancing vocational training, and integrating technology into the learning process to prepare students for the future.

  • Healthcare for All

    LDPI is committed to ensuring affordable and accessible healthcare for every citizen. We propose a comprehensive healthcare policy that emphasizes preventive care, mental health awareness, and strengthening public health infrastructure.

  • Cultural Progressivism

    We celebrate the diversity of Indian culture and support initiatives that promote inclusivity and social harmony. Our policies aim to safeguard the rights of minorities, promote gender equality, and support the arts and heritage.

  • Environmental Sustainability

    Our party recognizes the urgent need to address climate change and protect the environment. We advocate for sustainable development practices, investment in renewable energy, and conservation of natural resources.

  • Strong National Defense

    We support a robust national defense strategy that prioritizes the security of our borders and promotes strategic partnerships globally. Our policies emphasize technological advancements in defense and collaboration with friendly nations.

  • Secularism and Human Rights

    LDPI firmly believes in the principles of secularism and the protection of human rights. We are dedicated to creating policies that uphold the dignity and freedoms of all citizens, irrespective of their background.
